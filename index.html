Humble Souls was created to continue the work and legacy our Dad, Alan Dolan.
He had the kindest heart and cared about everyone he met.
His dream was to create a container village in Manchester for the homeless, helping anyone who needed help.

We want people to know that if you are unable to donate money you can donate your time, old clothes and food.
Even a smile to brighten someones day or words of encouragement when someone needs it the most, that was what our Dad was about.

Please visit our JustGiving page to donate or call 07503 002009 to arrange other forms of donation.
